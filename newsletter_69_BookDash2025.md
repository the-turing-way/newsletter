# Countdown to TTW November Book dash
![](https://assets.buttondown.email/images/5c0400b9-5c06-46cd-9844-738ef067dc18.png?w=960&fit=max)

<h1 style="text-align: center"><span style="font-family: monospace; color: rgb(67, 56, 202)">Save the date! The Turing Way Book Dash is taking place online from <strong>10-14 November 2025,<em> </em></strong>and <strong>APPLICATIONS ARE OPENING TODAY! ðŸŽ‰</strong></span><br></h1>

*The Turing Way* Book Dash is a collaborative event where you'll work with others to add to and improve *The Turing Way* book and become a part of its community. Read details about the Book Dash in *The Turing Way* [Community Handbook](https://the-turing-way.netlify.app/community-handbook/bookdash.html).

We will host online contribution sessions (collaborative co-working format) and at least one social networking session from **10-13 November 2025**, and two community share-out events on **14 November 2025**. [See details](https://the-turing-way.netlify.app/community-handbook/bookdash/bookdash-events.html#during-the-book-dash).

We have opened the call for application to participate in the upcoming Book Dash. The deadline for local hub submission is **10 October 2025**. **Application form for attendees:** [**https://forms.gle/LL5SN3RdGXVEzNbi7**](https://forms.gle/cD3qgrQggSveyKbL7)  
  
**We also have available our application form in Spanish, ECHALE UN OJO Y APUNTATE!** [**https://forms.gle/9L7bvUHAXDCR3J56A**](https://forms.gle/9L7bvUHAXDCR3J56A)

If you have previously interacted with the project or our community, and have always wanted to make more time to collaborate with us on your ideas, send us your application. Previous Book Dash attendees are also invited to express their interest to join us back as contributors, mentors for new participants and/or as a Book Dash Planning Committee member.

*You can use* [*the template document*](https://tinyurl.com/tw-bookdash-template) *to prepare your draft. Learn more about the* [*Book Dash and the application process*](https://the-turing-way.netlify.app/community-handbook/bookdash.html)*.*
